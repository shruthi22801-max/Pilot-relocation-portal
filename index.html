ADHOC Relocation Process
1
Pilot Submits Request
Status → Waitlisted · Auto-confirmation email sent
No transfer benefits for ADHOC. RLPL deducted from PL/CL.
2
Relocation Team — Preliminary check
Initial eligibility review · Pilot details verified & corrected if needed
Relocation team can edit Fleet, Designation, Base, Tenure, LRD before forwarding to AEP.
3
AEP Team Review
Eligibility & AEP clearance check · Status → In Process
AEP team verifies documents and provides tentative relocation month.
4
OCC Manpower Planning
Vacancy check at requested base · SWAPs arranged if needed
5
Relocation Team — Final Approval
Transfer order issued · Status → Approved / Rejected
AEP clearance mandatory before final approval.
6
Pilot Confirmation
Accept relocation date · RLPL adjustment processed
⚠You may withdraw at any stage before final approval. Cancellation after approval requires a mandatory reason.
